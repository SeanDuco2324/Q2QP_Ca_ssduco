Activities/Initiatives/Involvements of the Advocacy

In 1999, Greenpeace Philippines successfully campaigned for the passage of the Philippine Clean Air Act which provided a comprehensive air pollution control policy and included a national ban on waste incineration.

In 2001, Greenpeace Philippines also successfully campaigned for the passage of the Philippine Ecological Waste Management Act, which implements a comprehensive program for effective waste management which includes waste reduction, seperation and recycling.
