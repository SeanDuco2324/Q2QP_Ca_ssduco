Mission

Greenpeace's goal is to ensure the capability of Earth to nurture all life. Specifically, Greenpeace wishes to achieve the following:

  • Protect diversity in all its forms
  • Prevent pollution and abuse of the Earth's land, air, ocean and fresh water.
  • End all nuclear threats
  • Promote peace, global disarmament and non-violence.

How to contribute
